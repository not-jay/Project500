What is Project 500?

Project 500 is a green advocacy which promotes forest restoration and protection in Cebu.

Why?

This project aims to rehabilitate typhoon-devastated areas caused by the disastrous onslaught of Typhoon Yolanda, which left thousands of people homeless and vast areas of land barren.

When and Where?

The project will be conducted on April 4, 2014 to April 5, 2014 (Friday and Saturday) at Obo-ob Mangrove Garden Integrated Ecotourism and Conservation Association (OMAGIECA) in Bantayan Island, Cebu, Philippines. We will be planting 5000 mangroves for the cause!

How can I help?

We are inviting everyone to help in any way they can! It can be financial, or transportation, or even food! Like we said, in any way. You can even share this page and reach out to more people - that's already help! :D
